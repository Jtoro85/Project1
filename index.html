What I do.

To start this project, I might as well talk about my job. I manage a heart cath lab. I help people who have heart attacks. People have heart attacks because portion of their arteries become narrow.

When the vessel becomes very narrow or completely block, people will experience chest pain.

That is where my team and I come in.

Artery with narrow passage.

PCI

My team does a procedure called Percutaneous Coronary Intervention. That is a fancy way of saying that we put stents in the narraow vessel.
We inject contrast into the heart to see the bloackage. Then we know where to place the stent.

There is a bloakage in the Circumflex Artery.

Electrophisology

Another part of the job I do is called electrophysiology. This is a procedure involving a patient with a bad heart rhythm. We place special catheters in the patient's heart and then use them to ablate or BURN the piece of the heart causing the bad heart rhythm.

Alot of the work for the technician involves sitting at a computer till the heart rhythm is found by the doctor. It is a very time consuming procedure but rewarding a the end.
